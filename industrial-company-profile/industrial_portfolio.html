ProServ Industrial Ltd

Your single engineering partner for industrial doors, loading dock systems, and facility safety across Ireland and Europe.

When a building goes up, every opening in it matters. The loading docks where goods arrive. The doors that separate clean rooms from production floors. The barriers that protect people from moving vehicles. Each one has to work reliably, meet regulations, and last for decades.

That is what ProServ Industrial does. Since 2001, we have designed, supplied, installed, and maintained the access and safety systems that keep commercial and industrial buildings operational. From a single roller shutter replacement to a multi-bay loading dock fit-out on a new distribution centre, we deliver end-to-end engineering solutions — and we stay to maintain them.

One Company. Three Specialisations.

Most contractors in our sector cover one discipline. ProServ Industrial brings three together under one roof, one project team, and one service contract:

Industrial Door Systems

High-speed doors, sectional overhead doors, fire shutters, clean room doors, freezer doors, roller shutters, automatic gates. For every access point in your building.

Loading Dock Engineering

Dock levellers, vehicle restraints, dock shelters, scissors lifts, ramps. Complete loading bay design, supply, and installation as Ireland's Stertil Dock Products partner.

Facility Safety

Polymer impact barriers, bollards, guardrails, column protectors, handrails. Next-generation safety systems through our Boplan partnership.

Lifetime Service

Preventative maintenance programmes, emergency callouts, periodic compliance inspections, and authorised OEM parts supply. We maintain everything we install.
